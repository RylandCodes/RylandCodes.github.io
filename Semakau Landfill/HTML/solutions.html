Sustainable Solutions for All


Actions the Population Can Take

While the Singapore government has taken significant steps to manage waste, individual actions can make a difference in extending the lifespan of Semakau Landfill. A key action is reducing waste at the source. By choosing reusable over single-use items, individuals can reduce the amount of waste generated in their homes and businesses.

Additionally, waste segregation is essential. Singaporeâ€™s 3R (Reduce, Reuse, Recycle) initiative encourages everyone to separate recyclable materials such as paper, plastic, metal, and glass. By following this, individuals help reduce contamination, making recycling more effective and efficient.

Finally, public awareness campaigns play a crucial role. Educating others, especially younger generations, about sustainable waste management practices is key to ensuring that these solutions remain effective in the long term. Each person's effort, no matter how small, adds up, creating a larger collective impact in the fight against the landfill issue.
